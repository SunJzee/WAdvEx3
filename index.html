Introducing A new Technology

ImPossible !

In the exploration and production (E&P) sector, the process of locating hydrocarbons is an expensive, time-consuming, and expert-based speculative endeavor. This is primarily due to the absence of pre-existing knowledge about hydrocarbon presence in a targeted area.

However, there’s now a groundbreaking solution to overcome this hurdle, capable of identifying hydrocarbon deposits prior to the commencement of drilling. This method generates detailed maps showcasing areas rich in hydrocarbons and their subsurface variations with the utmost clarity that seismic data can offer.

 The effectiveness of this novel technology has been thoroughly validated and proven through its successful application in numerous field studies, including both 2D and 3D seismic surveys.

The advanced method is capable of simulating what are termed as “hydrocarbon signals,” which emerge from changes in properties of fluids and rocks, including saturation and viscosity. The intensity of these hydrocarbon signals is directly related to the amount of hydrocarbons present in the porous rocks below the surface.
